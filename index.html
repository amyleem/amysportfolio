About

I am a designer full of interests and passion for branding and motion graphics. During my years of experience in the design field, I have learned not only the skill of becoming a designer, but also its value and attitude. I am further developing my professionalism and open mind.

I am very interested in expressing my imagination with graphic design. I like to communicate with people, and I want to be a designer who shares my thoughts and creativity. My goal as a designer is to give people an innovative and positive message with my design philosophy.

Love Emotions Title Sequence

This project is creating a title sequence with a love story. My story is the feelings people have when they are in love. There are so many emotions that people feel while in love. Perhaps it is love that is at the center of our emotions. Everyone imagines romantic love at least once. Falling in love is an intense feeling that appears when you start to love.

Movie Taste Analysis

This video is a project that shows information as an info-graphic by creating a chart with figures from an app that analyzes my taste in movies. Since I was 17 years old, I’ve been writing and rating movies on this app, and I started this project with enough data.

Title Sequence inspired by Birds of Prey Movie

The title sequence feels energetic and vicious. It is dark, but it also gives an intense and funky feeling. Most of the illustrations used in the title sequence are vicious and sometimes scary, reflecting the violent content of the film.

Electronic Music Channel

The project is about creating kinetic typography video. I picked a electronic dance music. The name of song is “never be like you by flume.” The overall music sounds like sparking, stutter, and electronic sounds like robotics. It is like twisted techno that makes the tone of voice dreamy and fancy. This music reminds me a dreamy and little psychedelic graphics.

Cafe Branding

This project is branding a health spa cafe.The target group is urban adults aged 30-35 who want to enjoy their free time with relaxation and pampering. The cafe’s name is ‘Beauty and the City,’ which is inspired by the ‘Sex and The City’ that is a famous TV show.

Meet Dr.+ App

The Meet Dr.+ app is a platform where users and doctors can communicate and receive help together, and for those who have difficulty going to the hospital due to the influence of the pandemic, the hospital system is digitalized to create a better world.
